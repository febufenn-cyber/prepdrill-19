# Phase 11 Blind Spots

- Public availability is not the same as commercial indexing permission.
- Retired or disputed pages can remain in search caches unless metadata and sitemap state change deterministically.
- Unsigned deep links allow campaign spoofing and unsafe destinations.
- Guest attribution is easily lost or duplicated during account merge.
- Clicks and sign-ups can grow while meaningful activation and learning retention decline.
- Referral incentives invite self-referral, device farms, and repeated redemption.
- Experiments without predeclared metrics and guardrails create post-hoc storytelling.
- Marketing kill switches must stop page publication, outbound links, and experiment allocation independently.
- Real channel economics require production acquisition, retention, and payment evidence.
