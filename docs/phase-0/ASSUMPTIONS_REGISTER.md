# Assumptions Register

Phase 0 exists to convert these assumptions into evidence. An assumption is not closed because a document repeats it.

| ID | Assumption | Risk | Evidence required | Status |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| A01 | Serious UGC NET repeaters struggle to choose what to practise next | Critical | repeated behavioural evidence from 8–12 target interviews | open |
| A02 | A five-question diagnostic can create useful, honest next-action value | Critical | prototype tests showing users understand recommendation and uncertainty | open |
| A03 | Targeted drills feel more relevant than random mixed drills | Critical | blinded comparison with at least 70% targeted preference | open |
| A04 | Existing Paper 1 corpus can supply a trustworthy launch subset | Critical | 250-item audit and 100-item golden set | open |
| A05 | Per-question provenance and rights can be classified | Critical | source/rights review with no unresolved launch blockers | open |
| A06 | Controlled concept mapping is consistent enough for adaptation | High | representative sample mapped with reviewed disagreements | open |
| A07 | Learners value transparent evidence more than catalogue size | High | interviews and usability observations | open |
| A08 | Guest-first practice increases value without losing identity continuity | High | prototype preference and Phase 1/2 merge tests | open |
| A09 | Telegram, student networks or tutors can acquire qualified repeaters | High | controlled message smoke tests | open |
| A10 | Learners will pay for adaptation/improvement rather than only content | Medium | past purchase evidence and later reservation test | open |
| A11 | Canonical explanations plus constrained AI can preserve trust | High | later explanation evaluation against reviewed set | deferred |
| A12 | UGC NET Paper 1 creates a viable expansion path into Paper 2 | Medium | retention and cross-interest evidence after initial use | deferred |

## Status values

- `open`: untested or insufficient evidence
- `supported`: evidence currently supports the assumption
- `contradicted`: evidence rejects the assumption
- `mixed`: material evidence exists on both sides
- `deferred`: intentionally tested in a later phase

## Update rule

Every status change must include an evidence link, date, owner, and strongest contrary observation. Critical assumptions cannot be closed by founder judgement alone.
